KASARAGOD

Kasaragod is a municipal town and the district headquarters of Kasaragod district of Kerala state in India. Situated in the rich biodiversity of Western Ghats, it is known for the Chandragiri and Bekal Forts, Chandragiri River, historic Kolathiri Rajas, natural environment of Ranipuram and Kottancheri Hills, historical and religious sites like the Madiyan Kulom temple, Madhur Temple, Ananthapuram Lake Temple and Malik Deenar Mosque. It is located 50 km south of the major port city & a commercial hub Mangalore and 578 km north of state capital Thiruvananthapuram. Malayalam, Kannada and Tulu are the most widely spoken languages here, along with others like Karhade[only among Karhade Brahmins], Beary, Konkani are also used by some people. Malayalam spoken here is influenced by Tulu and Kannada and vice versa.
